Why does the material price, not change It might seem a bit odd at first glance, the price for a material, will always display per unit. And the price source will be determined by the priority explained below. This will also count for the intermediate craft and it's materials. Reagent cost selection priority

When a reagents cost is calculated, there are several scenarios to take into consideration. Is there a custom price? does one of the trade vendors yield a value for the item, does it exist at the AH? Is it priced above your defined max buyout versus market value percentage? and more.

So when it comes to giving an item a price value, they are prioritized as follows:

Custom price Trade vendor (TSM) Market value, if buyout is {{ getBuyoutLimit }}% or higher than the MV The currently lowest buyout, if the item is at the AH (TSM) Market value 0

The above values are then multiplied by the reagent count. In the reagent list however, the unit price is displayed instead.
